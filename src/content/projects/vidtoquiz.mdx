### Idea

Transform videos into engaging quizzes to enhance the learning experience.

### Tech Stack

- TypeScript
- Next.js
- React
- Supabase
- Shadcn
- tRPC

### Features

1. **Video to Quiz:**

   - Turn videos into interactive quizzes for a more engaging learning experience.

2. **Create Quiz from Docs:**

   - Transform PDFs, Docs, and Texts into captivating and dynamic interactive quizzes effortlessly.

3. **Efficient Question Generation:**

   - Create a versatile array of questions swiftly, optimizing your workflow and fostering efficiency in quiz creation.

4. **Export to Google Form:**
   - Effortlessly export quizzes to Google Forms, streamlining distribution for seamless accessibility to your audience.
